Design Internship 2021:

QUANTUM INTERFACE

I spent the summer of 2021 interning for a startup based in Austin, Texas called Quantum Interface. Quantum Interface specializes in vector-based predictive User Interface Design, which is mainly carried out in AR/VR systems and occasionally web and app development. As a Software Engineering and Design Intern, I familiarized myself with QI’s Javascript SDK for their technology called “QPoints” (there is an example of a QPoint on their website, linked above) and applied it to several websites.

I was able to participate in UX Design and Web Development during my internship. For example, I designed, developed, and applied a QPoint to the website for a nonprofit organization called Summits4Sight. I also designed several QPoints on Figma for a few other websites.

At my internship, I collaborated with QI’s UX Designer, Lead Project Manager, CTO, Lead Software Engineer, and DevOps Engineer in order to accomplish my tasks.
